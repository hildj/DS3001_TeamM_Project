**Data Summary**
The data was taken from a State of California dataset. It measured hospital mortality rates and quality ratings for different conditions. The data featured multiple years, from 2016-2023, and described different hospitals from different regions of California. 

**Provenance**
The data came from data.gov, and it was published from the State of California. It was last updated in November 23, 2025 and was created November 27, 2024.  The publisher is the Department of Health Care Access and Information and is maintained by Department of Health Care Access and Information, Information Services Division/Healthcare Analytics Branch. 
**License**
The data is public access and use. It has full Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 and is accessible by the public for use, especially education use like this project.
**Ethical Statements**
The data is fully anonymized and while it reveals hospital data, the data is publicly accessible and is meant to be viewable by everyone. As such, there is little harm or fear of creating a project regarding this data.
